What is ISU Grandprix?

ISU Grandprix is a series of 6 different senior international figure skating competition which leads to the Grandprix Final in December.
The competition is divided into 4 different divisons: mens' singles, ladies' singles, pair skating, and ice dancing.
Each division has two events: short program and long program.
